Day 1

Get a ~2 liter container. This will serve as a mixing and rising vessel.

Grab your scale and tare your container.

Fill your container with 360 grams of lukewarm water to which you will add 10 to 15 grams of salt.

Add ~100 grams of sourdough starter. (don't have any? No biggie! Why not make one?)

Mix well until the starter has completely dissolved.

Add ~500 grams of flour and mix until you have a dough that is mostly free of dry, floury spots.

Cover your container with cling-wrap so reduce moisture loss and set aside.

â° About 30 minutes later...

Drip some water over a clean surface and spread the water with your hands so that a surface about as big as a cutting board is uniformly soaked. Make sure your hands are also wet.

Take the dough out of its container.

Flatten the dough over the wet surface and fold in twice in half. Repeat this thrice.

After the third time, put the dough back in its container. It's ready to rise. Put the cling-wrap over your container.
